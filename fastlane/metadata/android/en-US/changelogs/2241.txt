v5.4.2: Mosh arrow keys work in mutt / emacs / less

- Mosh sessions now force DECCKM=on at connect, matching
  upstream mosh-client. Up/Down/Left/Right now send the
  application-mode SS3 escape sequences mutt, emacs, and
  less bind to their navigation keymaps. Fixes #73.
- Verified on-device: the client-side libvterm emits
  ESC O A for arrow keys from the very first byte of the
  mosh session, not ESC [ A as before.

v5.8.5: faster mosh session-end detection (#92)

Reduced the session-dead threshold from 15s to 8s.
The previous 15s timeout was correct but too slow —
users disconnected manually before it fired. With 8s,
the session closes on its own ~6s after typing `exit`.

Mosh-server sends keepalives every ~3s, so 8s of
silence (missing 2+ keepalives) reliably indicates
the server has exited without false-positives on
healthy networks.

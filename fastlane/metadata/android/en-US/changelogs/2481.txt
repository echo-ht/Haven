v5.15.0: quality + resolution controls in Convert

New Compression section inside the Convert dialog, tailored
at the main reason users come here — shrinking video.

• Quality (CRF) slider with an encoder-aware range. Lower =
  better, higher = smaller. Re-seeds to a sensible default
  when you switch between H.264, H.265, and VP9.
• Resolution dropdown: same as source, 4K, 1440p, 1080p,
  720p, 480p, 360p. Keeps aspect ratio, always even pixels.
• Encode speed preset (ultrafast → veryslow). Slower =
  smaller output at the same quality.
• Audio bitrate dropdown (64–320 kbps) for codecs that
  support it.
• Quick presets: Share 720p, Small 480p, Archive 1080p,
  HQ source.

The live CLI preview at the bottom of the dialog reflects
every knob in real time.

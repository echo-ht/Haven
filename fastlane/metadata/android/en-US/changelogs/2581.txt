v5.21.0-rc2: consolidated swipe gesture, page-jump fix (#89)

Builds on rc1's right-flick-navigates-up gesture. Two fixes
this round:

- Deduplicated the swipe handling. rc1 had the Files screen
  layering its own pointerInput on top of the pager's
  pagerSwipeOverride, which made the slow-vs-fast decision
  feel flaky — the two handlers were fighting for the same
  gesture. Now every horizontal drag on Terminal and Files
  flows through a single pagerSwipeOverride with an optional
  onFlick hook each screen can claim.

- Fixed a double-jump on normal tab swipes: because the
  pager's page index ticks over mid-drag once you've passed
  half a page, computing "next page = currentPage + 1" at
  release could skip two pages. Now anchored to the page held
  at finger-down so one swipe moves exactly one tab.

Please try normal tab swipes AND the fast right-flick in a
Files subdirectory, and feed back on #89.

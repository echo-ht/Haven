v5.24.2: F-Droid x86_64 wayland build fix

Bumps wayland-android submodule to work around a cairo meson probe
that only fails on the F-Droid buildserver for x86_64 (works for
arm64 on the same server, works for both ABIs locally). Cairo's
`cc.has_define('FT_LOAD_NO_SVG', ...)` probe escalates an internal
compile-test failure into a hard meson error instead of returning
false; patched in build-android.sh to use a freetype version gate
(equivalent to what cairo itself does for the internal-subproject
path).

No runtime change vs v5.24.1 — F-Droid packaging only.
